# 🫧 Bubble Popper

**Improving your cursor accuracy, one pop at a time.**

---

## 📝 Overview
https://youtu.be/SkDPFjviWnE demo video 
**Bubble Popper** is a fast-paced, web-based training tool designed to sharpen your clicking precision.  

Originally conceived during the **Vibe Coding Hackathon**, this project focuses on the **"Easy" module**, providing a solid foundation for reaction-time training and mouse control.

Whether you're a competitive gamer looking to improve your *flick shots* or just someone who wants smoother cursor navigation, this game is for you! 🎯✨

---

## ✨ Features

- 💥 **Satisfying Gameplay** – Click bubbles to pop them instantly  
- 🎯 **Precision Training** – Improves cursor control and spatial awareness  
- 🎨 **Minimalist UI** – Zero distractions, pure focus  
- 📈 **Live Scoring** – Real-time performance feedback  

---

## 🛠️ Why I Built This

During the **Vibe Coding Hackathon**, the challenge was to create modular difficulty levels (**Easy, Medium, Hard**).

Due to time constraints:

- ✅ **Easy Module** – Fully functional and polished  
- 🚧 **Medium/Hard Modules** – Designed conceptually for future implementation  

The goal was to bridge the gap between:
- Casual browsing  
- High-precision FPS gaming skills
